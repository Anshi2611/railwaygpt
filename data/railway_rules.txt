Indian Railways Refund Rules:
- If a confirmed ticket is cancelled more than 48 hours before departure, a small cancellation charge is deducted.
- If cancelled within 48 hours, higher charges apply.
- No refund is given after chart preparation.

Tatkal Rules:
- Tatkal booking opens at 10 AM for AC classes.
- Tatkal booking opens at 11 AM for Sleeper class.
- Tatkal tickets have limited refund.

Senior Citizen Concession:
- Male senior citizens above 60 years are eligible for concession.
- Female senior citizens above 58 years are eligible for concession.

Chart Preparation:
- Chart is prepared 4 hours before train departure.
- After chart preparation, cancellation rules change.
